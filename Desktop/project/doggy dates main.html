Doggy Dates provides pet
sitting, overnights, and play
dates “for the yuppie puppy!”



We’ve been taking care of dogs in the District Panchkula
since 2021– and we love it. We know how much
you adore your pets, so when work, vacations,
or schedule conflicts take you away from them,
you need a reliable dog walker and pet sitting 
service.
Doggy Dates Playgroup is the program that
made us famous. It provides a great energy
outlet for dogs, giving them the time and space
to run with friends and get the exercise they
need–but it’s indeed a lot more than that! Doggy
Dates Playgroup addresses the social essence of
dogs and their place in nature as “pack animals.”
Socialization and social interaction through
puppyhood and adolescence is vital to the
maturity of a healthy, happy, and confident adult
dog.
